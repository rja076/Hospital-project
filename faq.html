FAQ

you can asked your question here provide with answer

What are your hours of operation?
Our hospital is open 24 hours a day, 7 days a week. We are always here to provide the best possible care for our patients.
Do you accept insurance?
Yes, we accept a wide range of insurance plans. Our staff will be happy to help you understand your coverage and assist you with any insurance-related questions you may have.
What services do you offer?
We offer a wide range of medical services, including emergency care, surgery, physical therapy, diagnostic imaging, and pharmacy. Our team of highly skilled doctors and staff are dedicated to improving the health and well-being of our patients.
Can I request a specific doctor for my appointment?
Yes, you may request a specific doctor for your appointment. However, availability may vary and we cannot guarantee that your preferred doctor will be available for your appointment.
